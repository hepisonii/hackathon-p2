01
The Problem

Learning is expensive.
Talent goes unnoticed.

Millions of students and professionals want to grow their skills every day — but traditional classes are expensive, inflexible, and often disconnected from real-world needs.

At the same time, skilled individuals sit on goldmines of knowledge with no easy way to share it, teach it, or earn from it on their own schedule.

  • Formal education costs are rising faster than inflation
  • Skilled people have no simple platform to monetize their expertise
  • Learners and mentors in the same city can't find each other
  • Generic online courses feel impersonal and one-size-fits-all
02
The Solution

SkillSwap — where knowledge finds its match.

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer skill sharing and mentorship platform that directly connects learners with skilled mentors in their city and beyond — at a fraction of traditional costs.

Whether you want to learn guitar, crack coding interviews, master graphic design, or improve your public speaking — there's a mentor here for you.

03
How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Effective.

Getting started on SkillSwap takes less than 2 minutes. Here's how the journey looks from signup to your first session.

1
Create your profile Sign up as a Learner, Mentor, or both. Add your skills, bio, location and photo.
2
Browse or list skills Learners search by category, price, location & rating. Mentors create sessions with timings and pricing.
3
Book a session Pick a time slot, confirm your booking. Our system prevents double-bookings automatically.
4
Learn, teach & grow Complete your session and leave a rating. Track your learning journey on your dashboard.
04
Platform Features

Everything you need to learn and teach.

SkillSwap is built with features that make the experience smooth for both sides of the table.

  • Smart booking system with real-time availability — no overlapping sessions
  • Dual-role accounts — be a learner and a mentor simultaneously
  • Mentor dashboard with earnings overview and session tracking
  • Learner dashboard with upcoming sessions and full history
  • Session ratings and reviews to build trust in the community
  • Category filters — Programming, Design, Music, Languages & more
  • Location-based mentor recommendations for in-person sessions
  • Session status flow — Pending → Confirmed → Completed
